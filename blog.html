Murals in the Market (Eastern Market) September 2017

Murals in the Market occurs every year in September. The event takes place throught the Eastern Market in Detroit. Murals in the Market is made possible by the amazing people over at Inner State Gallery and 1xRun. Murals in the Market was created to allow artists from all over the world to express themselves through murals on abandoned or rundown buildings throughtout the market. This September Inner State Gallery and 1xRun will be presenting the 3rd Annual installment of Murals in the Market.

September of 2015, I had the pleasure of witnessing first hand Murals in the Market. As a self-proclaimed "artist" I really enjoyed this event. I had the pleasure of witnessing some of my favorite artists create masterpieces in not only my city but my neighborhood as well. Everyone was so inspiring and delightful to speak to and they weren't bothered by us spectators as we watched the process. I was so thrilled and intrigued with the concept and artwork that I chose to take some photographs for my dj business with the murals as my backdrop.
