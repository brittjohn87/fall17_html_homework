Who Is B?

Who is B? That is the question. Well I'm here to tell you that B is all things D (Detroit). I am a true Detroiter through and through, who enjoys living in the heartbeat of the city. For fun I spin tunes & create them whenever the opportunity presents itself. For a living I sell mortgages and God willing soon I'll be able to say I'm a developer for a fortune 500 company. When I'm not working I can assure you that I'm out enjoying the city in every way possible. I love bike riding through the city while discovering new hangouts and in the summer rooftop parties are my favorite. That's enough about me, go check out the Murals in the Market...please!
